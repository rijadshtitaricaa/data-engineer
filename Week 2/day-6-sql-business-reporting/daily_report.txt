# Day 6 - SQL Business Reporting Sprint

## Goal of the Practice

The goal of this practice was to move from simple SQL row-level queries into business reporting.

I worked with three related tables:

- orders
- customers
- products

The main focus was learning how SQL can answer business questions using:

- COUNT
- SUM
- AVG
- MIN
- MAX
- GROUP BY
- HAVING
- JOIN

The goal was not only writing SQL queries but understanding what the results mean from a business perspective.
